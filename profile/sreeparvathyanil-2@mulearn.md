- Sergio Ramos
- I know he didn’t play for Argentina, but he’s probably the player who made football look the most enjoyable to watch. The way he played felt effortless — skills, creativity, passing, everything looked natural. Even people who didn’t watch football regularly knew who he was because of how entertaining he was on the field.
